Indian Snacks

SAMOSA

Samosas are deep-fried pastries filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat. They are a popular street food in India.

MIRCHI BHAJI

Mirchi Bajji, more popularly known as Mirapakaya Bajji in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana is a popular street food snack made by batter frying green chilies.

ONION PAKODA

Make onion Pakoda that are delicately light, crunchy & crispy with a hint of natural sweetness and spiciness in every bite.

VEG CUTLET

Veg Cutlet made with mixed vegetables, herbs and spices makes for a great tea-time snack that your whole family will love!

ALOO TIKKI

Literally translating to Potato Patties, this Indian street food 'Aloo Tikki' is a staple at the street stalls, chaat centres and fancy cafes, getting sold out instanty.

CORN VADA

An easy Indian snack recipe made with sweet corn, lentils, spices and herbs that is loved worldwide.

Italian Snacks

Italian snacks are loved worldwide for their simple yet delicious flavors. Bruschetta, arancini, and panettone are some classic Italian snacks.

BRUSCHETTA

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer made of grilled bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil.

NACHOS WITH SALSA

Nachos with sweet and spicy salasa or pico de gallo sauce are famous for their crunchiness.

Mexican Snacks

Mexican snacks are known for their bold and spicy flavors. Tacos, nachos, and guacamole are some popular Mexican snacks.

TACOS

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various fillings like meat, vegetables, and salsa.

QUESADILLA

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, spices, and other fillings, and then cooked on a griddle or stove.

Japanese Snacks

Japanese snacks often combine sweet, savory, and umami flavors. Sushi, tempura, and onigiri are some well-known Japanese snacks.

SUSHI

Sushi is a popular Japanese dish made of vinegared rice, fish, and various toppings. It's often served with pickled ginger and wasabi.

KIMCHI

A wide selection of seasonings are used, including gochugaru, spring onions, garlic, ginger, and jeotgal, etc. Kimchi is also used in a variety of soups and stews.
